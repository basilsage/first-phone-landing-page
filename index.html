First Phone is building a simpler and safer phone for kids,
with no distractions and real privacy.

We believe young people deserve tech that keeps them connected to the people who care about them — without hijacking their attention or selling their data. We're building a dumb phone (New York Times) with core features like calling, messaging, and GPS, but without social media and open internet access. Everything is end-to-end encrypted, with no data collection or ads. Ever.

Read our prospectus for more product details.

The project is led by Ryan and DJ, and inspired by leading dumb phone projects like Light Phone and Wise Phone.
